About Us

Located in Batam, Indonesia, Carica is an inviting 40 seat restaurant owned and operated by accomplished Chef Iwan Sunito. "Like you, I enjoy food that satisfies the soul as well as the palate," says Iwan. "Food excites the imagination along with the taste buds. So, I invite you to experience food that holds the promise of a good story!"

Carica offers exceptional gourmet Asia Cuisine with an continental flair - served in a warm atmosphere where the emphasis is on comfort, taste and above all, relaxation and enjoyment. Grilled NY Strip with Baked Mac n Cheese and Sweet Onion Jam laced Demi-Glace; Angelhair pasta with Shrimp, diced tomatoes, spinach, roasted red peppers & lemon vodka cream sauce; and Grilled Salmon with farro & cranberry salad with mustard sauce are just a few of the special dishes you'll find on Carica's enticing menu. Cap off your main course with a Cappuccino and one of our specialty desserts such as Banana Napoleon or Creme Brulee. Enjoy a glass of fine wine, or indulge in a specialty martini or after-dinner cordial.

At Carica, we turn the ordinary into the extraordinary every day. So, stop in for a romantic dinner, relaxing lunch, or after work gathering at our cozy, sit-down bar...make everyday life a little more special at Carica Restaurant!

Award-winning, generous, unpretentious and prepared to perfection. If you’re serious about food, a visit to the highly acclaimed Carica Restaurant is a must. Open since 1991 the Crab offers a wide variety of gourmet dishes, from fresh local seafood to traditional Asia fare.
